Topic Guide

This page contains a list of guides on special topics the are not covered in the Dillo manual. While the manual puts the main focus on the browser and its features, some other topics require additional pieces of software so they are better covered in an different place.

Playing multimedia

While Dillo cannot play video or audio directly from the browser, it can easily offload this task to other programs. This article covers how to setup mpv with yt-dlp to be able to watch YouTube videos or listen to Bandcamp albums by opening the URLs directly from Dillo.
